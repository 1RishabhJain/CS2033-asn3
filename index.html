Our Business

ErgoSpace specializes in high quality and affordable ergonomic office equipment to make your workstation a productive and comfortable working area. We offer many types of desks, chairs, and desk accessories to cater precisely to your needs. Our products are suitable for use in a home office or commercial space and are backed by a 3-year warranty. Our online catalogue has excellent prices and a variety of choices on the latest high-tech equipment. ErgoSpace has fast and free shipping on everything you need to create your workspace.




The ErgoSpace Team

Andrea Piacquadio - CEO

Andrea Piacquadio is ErgoSpace’s founder and current CEO. He manages the business as a whole and ensures smooth operation. Piacquadio is the main link between the divisions of the company and sometimes handles large corporate deals.

Robert Murphy - Sales & Marketing

Robert Murphy is ErgoSpace’s sales and marketing lead. He oversees the sales and marketing side of the business and works closely with the designers. Murphy keeps himself up to date with the latest trends to market our products correctly.

Katie Mazzella - Designer

Katie Mazzella, ErgoSpace’s head designer, envisions all the products that ErgoSpace releases. Mazzella manages a team that specializes in the design of ergonomic office equipment. She often runs tests to ensure the products are comfortable and help create good posture.
